Navegaon Zone

About Navegaon Safari Gate

Navegaon offers a pristine forest experience with exceptional opportunities for tiger sightings.

Navegaon is one of the most scenic entry gates into Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve, characterised by its dense dry deciduous forests and abundant water bodies. Located on the eastern boundary of the reserve, this zone offers a more secluded safari experience with rich biodiversity that rivals any other zone in the park.

The forests of Navegaon are dominated by teak, bamboo, and ain trees, creating a dense canopy that shelters a thriving wildlife population. Several perennial water bodies within the zone ensure that animals are present throughout the year. The Navegaon zone connects to the Tadoba lake area, creating a large contiguous habitat that supports tigers, leopards, wild dogs, and a spectacular variety of birdlife.

Tigress Babli is the most famous resident of the Navegaon buffer area, regularly seen during evening safaris. The zone also sees a healthy population of sloth bears and gaurs. Visit the Navegaon safari zone page for more details on the wildlife of this area.

Zone Highlights

Why Explore the Navegaon Zone?

Navegaon's pristine forests and abundant wildlife offer a truly immersive Tadoba experience.

Rich Biodiversity

Navegaon supports a dense population of tiger prey species including chital, sambar, and wild boar. This healthy prey base sustains a thriving predator population.

Permanent Water Sources

Unlike zones that dry up in summer, Navegaon has perennial water bodies that attract wildlife year-round, making it reliable even during peak summer.

Buffer Zone Access

Navegaon offers both core and buffer zone safaris. The buffer areas are particularly productive for wildlife sightings and provide a more flexible booking experience.

Avian Diversity

The dense canopy and water bodies attract over 200 bird species. Navegaon is a paradise for birdwatchers with resident and migratory species throughout the season.

Expert Tracking

Our naturalists have deep knowledge of the Navegaon zone and its resident wildlife. Their tracking skills ensure rewarding safaris for guests at the best resort in Tadoba.

Serene Environment

Navegaon receives fewer visitors, offering a tranquil safari experience. The dense forest creates a sense of being completely immersed in the wild.

Famous Tigers

Resident Tigers of Navegaon Zone

The Navegaon zone hosts magnificent tigers that make every safari memorable.

Tigress Babli

Tigress Babli is the most famous resident of the Navegaon buffer area. Known for her graceful movements and regular appearances during evening safaris, she has become a favourite among wildlife photographers. Babli's consistent presence makes Navegaon a reliable zone for tiger sightings.

The Royal Bengal Tigers

Navegaon's dense forests and abundant prey support a healthy population of Royal Bengal Tigers. The zone is part of the core area of Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve and contributes to the overall tiger density of over 197 individuals across the 625 square kilometre reserve.
