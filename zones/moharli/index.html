Moharli Zone

About Moharli Safari Gate

Moharli is the primary entry point for Tadoba National Park safaris, offering exceptional wildlife viewing opportunities year-round.

Moharli stands as the busiest and most productive entry gate for Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve. Located on the western boundary of the park, this zone offers the highest probability of tiger sightings among all Tadoba safari zones. The landscape is defined by dense teak forests, expansive grasslands, and several crucial water bodies that attract wildlife throughout the year.

The Irai Lake, situated within the Moharli range, acts as a permanent water source that sustains an extraordinary concentration of wildlife. Tigers, leopards, sloth bears, wild dogs, and gaurs are regularly sighted along its shores. During the summer months, the receding water levels create prime viewing opportunities as animals congregate around the remaining water bodies.

Moharli is the home range of several famous Tadoba tigers including the legendary Tigress Maya, also known as the Lady of Tadoba. Her lineage has dominated this zone for over a decade. Other notable residents include Tigress Bijliee, whose calm demeanour makes her a favourite among photographers.

Zone Highlights

Why Choose Moharli for Your Safari?

The Moharli zone offers distinct advantages that make it the preferred choice for most visitors.

Exceptional Tiger Density

Moharli boasts the highest tiger density among all Tadoba zones. Multiple resident tigers including Maya, Bijliee, and their cubs are sighted regularly.

Irai Lake Proximity

The large Irai Reservoir within the zone attracts wildlife year-round. Summer months offer spectacular sightings as animals gather around shrinking water bodies.

Best Season

Moharli delivers excellent sightings throughout the safari season from October to June. March to May offers the best tiger viewing near water sources.

Diverse Birdlife

Over 200 bird species have been recorded in this zone. From Crested Serpent Eagles to Indian Pittas, birdwatchers find Moharli exceptionally rewarding.

Expert Naturalists

Our guides at the best resort in Tadoba have extensive experience in the Moharli zone. Their knowledge of tiger territories and animal behaviour ensures rewarding safaris.

Safari Variety

Both core zone and buffer zone safaris are available through Moharli. This flexibility allows visitors to choose between different wildlife experiences.

Famous Tigers

Resident Tigers of Moharli Zone

The Moharli range is home to some of Tadoba's most iconic tigers, regularly sighted by guests at the best resort in Tadoba.

Tigress Bijliee

Tigress Bijliee is one of the most photographed residents of the Moharli zone. Her calm disposition and regular presence near the safari routes make her a highlight for visitors. Bijliee is frequently seen with her cubs, offering unforgettable family sightings.

King Matkasur

King Matkasur is the dominant male tiger of the Moharli region. His imposing build and expansive territory make him a regular sighting. Matkasur's presence ensures a well-balanced ecosystem and provides spectacular viewing opportunities during Tadoba safari packages.
