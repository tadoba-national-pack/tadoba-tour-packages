Famous Tigers of Tadoba

Meet the Royal Residents of Tadoba National Park

Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve is home to some of India's most celebrated wild tigers. These magnificent cats define the wilderness of this ancient forest.

Tigress Bijliee

Among the most photographed tigresses in the reserve, Bijliee is a regular presence in the Moharli zone, known for her striking features and calm demeanour.

King Matkasur

The dominant male of Tadoba, Matkasur commands a vast territory. His imposing presence makes every sighting a highlight for visitors on Tadoba safari packages.

Bajrang

A powerful male tiger frequently encountered across the Kolara and Navegaon zones. Bajrang's movements are closely tracked by our guides.

Tigress Babli

A graceful resident of the buffer areas, Babli is often sighted during evening safaris. Her regular appearances delight photographers.

Chota D

An emerging male tiger gradually establishing his territory. Early morning safari guests have the best chance of encountering him.

The Royal Bengal Tigers

Tadoba shelters over 197 tigers within its 625 square kilometres, offering one of the highest tiger densities in India. Book the best resort in Tadoba for a chance to see them in the wild.
