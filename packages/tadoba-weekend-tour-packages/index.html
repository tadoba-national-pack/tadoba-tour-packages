Tour Description

Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve stands as Maharashtra's oldest and largest national park, a landscape of dry deciduous forests punctuated by large water bodies that sustain an extraordinary density of wildlife. This weekend package is designed for those who want a concentrated dose of wilderness without needing an extended leave from work.

Over two safaris, you will explore the reserve's most productive zones alongside naturalists who know the terrain intimately. The lakes of Tadoba, including Irai, Moharli, Telia, and Tadoba, act as magnets for wildlife, drawing in not only the famous tigers but also leopards, sloth bears, wild dogs, and a spectacular array of birdlife. Famous resident tigers like Maya, Choti Tara, and King Matkasur are regularly sighted in these areas.

Accommodation at Tadoba Safari Stay ensures you are well-rested and well-fed between excursions. All meals are prepared fresh, and the evening hours are spent around a bonfire sharing stories of the day's sightings. This is the ideal choice for a Tadoba weekend tour package that balances adventure with relaxation.

We invite you to explore the full range of Tadoba tour packages and plan your visit to the finest Tadoba tour safari experience available.

Itinerary

Day 1 - Arrival and Evening Safari

Pick-up from Nagpur and drive to Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve, a scenic journey of approximately three hours covering 140 km.

Check-in at Tadoba Safari Stay resort and settle into your cottage.

Evening safari session with an experienced guide.

Bonfire and dinner at the resort. Overnight stay.

Day 2 - Full-Day Safari Experience

Early morning safari (6:00 AM - 10:00 AM) when wildlife activity is at its peak.

Return to the resort for a hearty breakfast and rest.

Afternoon lunch followed by leisure time.

Evening safari (3:00 PM - 6:00 PM) exploring a different zone of the reserve.

Dinner and overnight stay at the resort.

Day 3 - Departure

Breakfast at the resort.

Check-out and transfer back to Nagpur.

Tiger Sightings in Tadoba

The Moharli zone is one of the best areas in Tadoba for tiger encounters. Famous residents like Tigress Bijliee and King Matkasur are regularly sighted here, especially near the Irai and Telia water bodies.
