Tour Description

Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve is the largest and oldest national park in Maharashtra, celebrated for its breathtaking landscapes and exceptional tiger density. Four safaris over three days provide the most thorough exploration of this magnificent forest, covering multiple zones and vastly improving your chances of encountering tigers, leopards, and other wildlife.

Beyond the Bengal Tiger, Tadoba supports leopards, sloth bears, Indian bison (gaur), wild dogs, and over 200 species of birds. The park's diverse topography of teak forests, bamboo thickets, and expansive grasslands creates a dynamic environment where every safari brings something new. Famous resident tigers such as Maya, Choti Tara, and King Matkasur are among the most sought-after sightings.

This Tadoba Safari Package allocates safaris across different zones including Kolara, Navegaon, Moharli, and Kolsa, subject to availability. Each zone offers a distinct character and set of species. Our Tadoba Safari Booking Package is designed for maximum coverage of the reserve's best habitats.

Itinerary

Day 1 - Arrival and First Safari

Pick-up from Nagpur and drive to Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve.

Check-in at the resort and preparation for the first safari.

Afternoon/evening safari with an experienced naturalist guide.

Bonfire and dinner. Overnight stay at the resort.

Day 2 - Two Safaris Across Different Zones

Early morning safari (best window for tiger sightings).

Return to resort for breakfast and mid-day rest.

Afternoon lunch at the resort.

Evening safari in a different zone of the reserve.

Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3 - Final Safari and Departure

Early morning safari for one last encounter with the wild.

Return to the resort for breakfast.

Check-out and transfer back to Nagpur.

All logistics from permits to meals are handled so you can focus entirely on the wildlife. With accommodation at the best resort in Tadoba, your comfort is assured between each excursion.
