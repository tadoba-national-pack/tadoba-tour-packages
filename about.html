About Tadoba Safari Stay

The story behind the best resort in Tadoba National Park

Our Story

Welcome to Tadoba Safari Stay

Tadoba Safari Stay was born from a simple belief that the best wildlife experiences should never compromise on comfort. As a premier resort in Tadoba National Park, we bridge the gap between raw wilderness and restful accommodation, offering guests a base from which to explore Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve without sacrificing the little luxuries that make a holiday memorable.

Our location places us within easy reach of the Moharli, Kolara, and Navegaon entry gates. Each gate opens into a distinct landscape within the reserve, giving our guests access to a variety of habitats and wildlife sightings. The property itself sits amidst the dry deciduous forests that define this region, with the large water bodies of Irai Lake and Telia Lake sustaining a rich ecosystem just beyond our boundaries.

Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve spans 625.4 square kilometres and shelters more than 197 tigers, alongside leopards, sloth bears, Indian bison, and over 200 bird species. The ancient forests here have stood for centuries, and the largest national park in Maharashtra continues to be a stronghold for the Royal Bengal Tiger.

Our team draws on decades of combined experience in wildlife tourism. From naturalists who can read the forest to hospitality professionals who anticipate your needs, every member of our staff shares a deep respect for the natural world.

The Resort

Tadoba Accommodation at Its Finest

Experience the best tadoba accommodation with premium amenities surrounded by nature.

Our cottages and rooms are designed with the wildlife traveller in mind. After a day spent tracking tigers across the reserve, returning to a comfortable space with modern amenities is essential. Each room balances rustic charm with the practical comforts of home.

Resort amenities include:

  • Luxury cottages with attached bathrooms and hot water
  • Multi-cuisine restaurant serving local Maharashtrian and Indian dishes
  • Swimming pool for relaxation between safaris
  • Bonfire evenings and wildlife documentaries
  • Pick-up and drop services from Nagpur

The Nagpur to Tadoba distance is approximately 140 km, making it a manageable drive of about three hours through the Maharashtra countryside. We also provide information for guests travelling from other cities about the Pune to Tadoba distance and Mumbai to Tadoba options.
